# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want ERouter to be a welcoming project for everyone: study groups,
indie developers, local community maintainers, and anyone curious about
APIs and gateways.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful, assume good faith, focus on the work.
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully.
- Prioritise the community's goals over individual ego.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, insults, personal attacks.
- Public or private aggression, intimidation, or stalking.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code,
issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may
be reported to the maintainers via a GitHub issue marked `private` or
through the contact channel listed in `SECURITY.md`.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, v2.1.
